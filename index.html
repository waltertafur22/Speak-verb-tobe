📚 The Verb "To Be"
Three magic words: AM · IS · ARE!
🙋
AM
Use with: I
I am a student.
I am a doctor.
👤
IS
Use with: He / She / It
She is a teacher.
He is a chef.
👥
ARE
Use with: You / We / They
They are farmers.
We are students.

Hi! My name is Tomás. I am a student. My school is near the beautiful Magdalena River 🌊 in Neiva. My best friend is Valentina. She is a student too!
My dad is a doctor 👨‍⚕️. He works at the big hospital. My mom is a teacher 👩‍🏫. She teaches at our school! We are a happy family. We love eating asado huilense 🍖 on Sundays!
After school, Valentina and I are at the park ⚽. Our friends are soccer players! In Neiva, the weather is very warm ☀️. We are so happy here!
