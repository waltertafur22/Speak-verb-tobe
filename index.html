Ability · Permission · Possibility
FormExample

❌ NegativeShe can't speak French.

Should / Shouldn't
Advice · Recommendations
FormExample
✅ AffirmativeYou should eat vegetables.
❌ NegativeYou shouldn't stay up late.
❓ QuestionShould I study tonight?

Choose the correct answer:

"You _____ drink lots of water every day."

Must / Mustn't
Obligation · Rules · Prohibition
FormExample
✅ AffirmativeYou must wear a seatbelt.
❌ NegativeYou mustn't run in school.
❓ QuestionMust I finish this today?

Choose the correct answer:

May / May not
Formal Permission · Possibility
FormExample
✅ AffirmativeYou may use a dictionary.
❌ NegativeYou may not use your phone.
❓ QuestionMay I leave early today?

Weak Possibility · Uncertainty
FormExample
✅ AffirmativeIt might rain later.
❌ NegativeShe might not come today.
❓ QuestionMight this be the answer?

📖 Reading

The Magical Adventure Camp

Read the story and find all the modal verbs!

Full text transcript available below in the story.
🏕️🌲✨🌟🦁

It was the first day at Magical Adventure Camp. The camp director stood in front of all the children and said:


"Welcome, everyone! You must listen carefully to all the rules. First, you must always wear your safety helmet when climbing. You mustn't go near the river alone — it can be very dangerous!"


Little Maya raised her hand. "Can we go swimming?" she asked. "Yes, you can swim, but only when a teacher is watching," answered the director. "And you should always swim with a buddy."


🏊‍♀️🧢🦅

In the afternoon, the children went on a nature walk. Their guide told them: "You shouldn't touch any plants you don't recognise — some might be dangerous. But don't worry, you might see some amazing animals!"


Suddenly, Tom spotted something in the trees. "Look! That might be an eagle!" he shouted. The guide smiled. "You may take photographs, but you mustn't make loud noises — it might fly away!"


🦅📸🌙

That night, the children sat around the campfire. "May I tell a story?" asked Sara. "Of course!" said the director. "You should all listen — it might be the best story you've ever heard!"


Everyone laughed. It was the most magical day ever. They can't wait for tomorrow!

Transcript:
